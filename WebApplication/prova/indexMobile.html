IoT platform for smart organs delivery

One of the most critical aspects of organ transport for transplantation is the lack of information on the conditions of transport. In particular, the proposed system envisages monitoring the condition of the organ in order to improve the management and increase the reliability of delivery. The system offers various services dedicated to measuring the temperature of the environment in which the organ is contained, measuring the vibrational state, tracking and delivery time.
