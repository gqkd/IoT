IoT platform for smart organs delivery

One of the most critical aspects of organ transport for transplantation is the lack of information on the conditions of transport. In particular, the proposed system envisages monitoring the condition of the organ in order to improve the management and increase the reliability of delivery. The system offers various services dedicated to measuring the temperature of the environment in which the organ is contained, measuring the vibrational state, tracking and delivery time. Access to data relating to the state of the organ’s transportation is carried out by the actors at different levels, signing the box in which the organ to be transplanted will be contained. After the subscription, it will be possible to view the above mentioned data sent from the box. Functionalities provided by the system to the stakeholders: 1) The surgical team of the receiving hospital has at its disposal a telegram bot to receive notifications when the box is about to arrive at the hospital to start preparing survey procedures. Moreover, it has access to box’s information via the web app. 2) The healthcare workers of the receiving hospital will be able to monitor the temperature trend, the vibrational state, the position in real time and the time elapsed since the organ was placed in the box via web app. While the Healthcare national system is able to retrieve data from all the boxes used at a specific time. 3) The transport team will be alerted by telegram message and by an acoustic signal emitted from the box if the temperature and vibrations are outside the safety range. Moreover, it has access to box’s information via the web app. Summarizing, the main feature the system offers are: - Telegram bot for notifications - Web app for end-users interface - Unified interfaces (through REST and MQTT) available to enable Demand/Response
